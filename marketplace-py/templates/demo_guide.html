Suggested walkthrough
Browse anonymously
Go to /browse/ — see a lively marketplace with real jobs from real organizations
Log in as carlos_otomi
Dashboard shows his applications and active work. His profile has audio. Go to his submitted work to see/hear the election spot recording.
Log in as demo_funder
Owner dashboard shows jobs at every stage. Go to the ecoturismo job — see Carlos's submission with audio. Play it. Accept or reject.
Log in as diego_aymara
See his draft submission on the children's story — work in progress state.
Switch language to Otomi
Use the language selector. Audio buttons throughout the UI will play Otomi narration for interface elements. This is the accessibility-first design in action.
